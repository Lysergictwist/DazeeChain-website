The DazeeChain Ecosystem

DazeeCoin (DZ)

Native cryptocurrency built on Polygon for low fees and fast transactions. DZ incentivizes responsible pet ownership and supports animal welfare.

  • 1 Billion DZ total supply
  • Built on Polygon (Ethereum L2)
  • Deflationary tokenomics
  • Real-world utility focused

PawPrintz

Blockchain-powered digital record system that securely stores and manages your pet's complete identity and health history on our private blockchain or locally on your device.

  • Verified adoption records
  • Secure vet history
  • Vaccination tracking
  • Owner-controlled sharing
  • Dual storage options (blockchain/local)
